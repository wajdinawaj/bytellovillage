Welcome to Bytello village

01.23.09

Bitello village: A Charming Oasis in the Lap of Nature Just steps away from the city of Ramallah, Bytello village is located as a green jewel that adorns the slopes of the mountains and dazzles onlookers with its stunning beauty. The village rises about 550 meters above sea level and has a large area estimated at 13,418dunums. It is surrounded by the villages of Nabi Saleh, Kubar, Al-Mazraa al-Qibliyya, Deir Ammar, Deir Abu Mishal, and Deir Nizam, forming a stunning natural painting. Bytello has a long history dating back to the Roman era, where it was known as “Alon,” which is a corruption of its original name, “Beit Ilu,” which means “House of God.” The village is full of archaeological sites and ancient stories told from generation to generation.
